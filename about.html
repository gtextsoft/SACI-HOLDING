About Saci Holdings

Saci Holdings, LLC is a private equity and investment management firm headquartered in Houston, Texas. We specialize in acquiring, developing, and scaling high-growth businesses, with a core focus on artificial intelligence (AI) and technology-enabled enterprises.

As a global holding company, we own a diverse portfolio of operating subsidiaries across real estate, media, and event production. Our long-term strategy is to build sustainable, scalable, and sector-leading businesses across multiple industries and continents.

Beyond acquiring technology-driven companies, we also acquire businesses that are not yet leveraging AI or digital transformation and integrate these tools to modernize, scale, and reposition them as competitive, future-ready organizations.
