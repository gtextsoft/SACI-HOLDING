About Dr. Stephen Akintayo

Chairman, SACI Holdings | Global Investment Expert | Forbes Business Council

Dr. Stephen Akintayo is a Forbes Business Council member and the Chairman of SACI Holdings, a global private equity firm transforming industries across Africa, the UAE, the UK, and the USA. Rising from humble beginnings to become a Harvard-trained investment expert and author of 45+ books, he leverages his multi-sector ecosystem spanning real estate, tech, and education to bridge the gap between luxury and economic empowerment. Through his foundation and university, Dr. Akintayo is dedicated to a legacy-driven mission of raising one million millionaires by 2050.
